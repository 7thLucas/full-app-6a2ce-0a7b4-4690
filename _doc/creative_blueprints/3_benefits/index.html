Every School Morning

The Routine, Step by Step

From alarm to front door — Morning Buddy guides every step independently.

⏰

Wake Up

Full-screen alarm fires at the set time. Child taps "I'm Awake!" to dismiss.

🚿

Wash Up

Bath or wash up — first step in the guided checklist, large text and emoji.

👕

Get Dressed

Uniform on. Star trail progress bar fills as each step is completed.

🍳

Breakfast

Eat breakfast — timed step with a friendly duration hint shown on screen.

📚

Study Check

Quick homework or bag review before heading out — no last-minute panic.

🎉

All Done!

Confetti celebration fires. Child is out the door — on time, every time.

Every step is customisable by the parent.

Add, remove, reorder, or rename any step — change the emoji, title, and duration hint from the parent setup screen.
