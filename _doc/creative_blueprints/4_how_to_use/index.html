Getting Started

Set Up Once.
Runs Every Morning.

Three steps for parents. Zero steps every morning after that.

1

Set the Alarm

Open parent setup (PIN-protected), enter your child's name, pick the wake-up time, and choose which days the alarm fires — weekdays only or a custom schedule.

⏰ Full-screen alarm with "I'm Awake!" button

2

Build the Routine

Add each morning step — bath, get dressed, breakfast, study review. Set the emoji, title, and duration hint for each. Drag to reorder. Remove anything that doesn't fit your child's morning.

📋 Fully customisable — add, remove, reorder

3

Hand It to Your Child

That's it. Every morning the alarm fires, your child taps through each step independently, earns stars along the way, and hits the confetti screen when they're done.

🎉 Parent checks the 7-day dashboard — no nagging needed
