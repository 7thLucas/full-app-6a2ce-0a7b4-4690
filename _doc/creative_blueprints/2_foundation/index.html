The Problem

is a battle.

    Parents repeat the same instructions every single day — wake up, get dressed, eat, pack your bag.

  • —

    Children move slowly, get distracted, and lose track of what comes next.

The Solution

Rise & Ready guides
kids independently.

  • ✓

    Smart alarm wakes the child at the right time with a friendly, kid-appropriate sound — weekdays only.

  • ✓

    Step-by-step checklist walks them through every morning task — wash up, get dressed, brush teeth, eat, study review, pack bag, shoes on — tap each step to complete it.

  • ✓

    Parent dashboard shows whether today's routine was completed — no nagging, no guessing.
